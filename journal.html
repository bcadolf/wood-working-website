Now I am no longer a child or teenager. Time is a lot harder to come by when you have a job and a child. My projects have also changed in scale. As must as I wish to sit and slowly carve away, power tools same time, and allow me to feel like I make progress on my projects even if I only have a few minutes to do something. My passion for woodworking is still there the projects have changed and will continue to change just as I am changing.
